The black dashed line separate the physical (first 8) and transient modes ( 9 to 30). since \[ u(x_{n},t)=\!\!\sum_{k=-N/2+1}^{N/2}\!\!a_{k}(t)e^{iq_{k}x_{n}} \] Denote \(a_k = b_k + ic_k\), so we has state space \([b_1, c_1, b_2, c_2, \cdots]\). X-axis is the exponent index of this state space
